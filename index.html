About Us

Join Study works for skill development in the field of logistics & supply chain industries. We are working across the world and we have students from different parts of world. Our trained and certified students are doing well in the profession.

Join Study works for skill development in the field of logistics & supply chain industries and founded by Ajit Kumar Mishra, CMILT. He is Chartered Member of "The Chartered Institute of Logistics & Transport". Dedicated himself for educational and skill development of logistics & supply chain professionals. He is a Chartered Supply Chain Analyst. Mr. Mishra is educational associates and faculty memeber of some management institutions and colleges.
